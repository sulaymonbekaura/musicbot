🎵 Music Finder Bot

Music Finder Bot is a Telegram bot that allows users to identify and find music from audio or video files. Users send a song, and the bot returns its title, artist, and other details.

🚀 Features

Works directly on Telegram

Identifies music from audio and video files

Fast and convenient responses

Supports multiple platforms (ACRCloud, Shazam, AudD APIs)

Compatible with Python 3.8+
